Italian Cuisine

Lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta made in flat and wide sheets, it originates from italian cuisine and served
in many forms. baked from wide flat noodles layered with meat, cheese and sauce, it comes from
Ancient Italy and is a rich and savoury dessert

Thai Cuisine

Pad See Ew

A stir fried noodle recipe with thick noodles. Pad see ew means “stir fried soy sauce noodles” It’s a popular street food meal.
Pad See Ew combines Cantonese stir fry style with Thai flavors and mixes in Chinese Broccoli.
It is a mix of cultures, with the dish being altered to fit into local tastes by substituting original ingredients with local ones instead.

Tom Yom Goong

Goong in Tom Yung Goong the Goong just means shrimp so this is a shrimp soup.
A traditional Thai Hot and Sour Shrimp Soup, it originates from the Thai central plains where the ingredients are.
The more modern recipe used emerged in the 20th century though, changing from a local nourishment into a globally recognized recipe.
